Diagon Alley

You are whisked off to a seedy alley somewhere in London, but not the modern city you are familiar with. It looks like something out of a medieval movie set with ancient buildings and oddly dressed people.

Signs over many shop windows indicate the name but not always the purpose of each mysterious establishment:

  • Gringotts Bank
  • Ollivanders
  • Eeylops Owl Emporium & Magical Menagerie
  • Flourish and Blotts
  • Madam Malkin's Robes for All Occassions
  • Quality Quidditch Supplies
  • Florean Fortescue's Ice Cream Parlour
  • Twilfitt Tatting
  • Slug & Jiggers Apothecary
